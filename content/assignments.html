Preparation

You will get the most out of this class if you (1) attend class, (2) complete all the readings, and (3) engageTake detailed notes, do practice exercises, ask for help on the Slack

 with the readings.

Homework

To practice coding in R you will complete 7 problem sets throughout the semester. You need to show that you made a good faith effort to work each question. Grades will be some function of effort + correct answers. More to come on this.

Feel free to work together on the problem sets (in fact, you should) but you must turn in your own answers. You cannot work in groups of more than three people, and you must note who participated in the group in your assignment.
